Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

  • How do I create an account?
    Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen and enter your email and name. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.
  • How do I log my first dream?
    Tap the "Log Dream" button on the home screen. You can type or use voice-to-text to record your dream.
  • What are reality checks?
    Reality checks are techniques to help you become aware when you're dreaming. Set up reminders in the app to practice throughout the day.
